About WattWise

WattWise was born from a simple question: How might we empower Washington homeowners to better understand and act on the financial savings and environmental benefits of optimizing their utility usage?

We believe that every homeowner deserves access to clear, evidence-based recommendations that make sustainable living both achievable and rewarding. Whether you're a first-time homeowner eager to reduce your carbon footprint or a busy parent looking to lower utility bills while modeling eco-friendly habits for your family, WattWise provides the insights and tools you need to make informed decisions.

Our platform transforms complex energy data into actionable steps, helping you understand not just what to change, but why it matters for your wallet and the planet.
